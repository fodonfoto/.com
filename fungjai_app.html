New Feature Fungjai Application

Origin of the concept

When mobile is another important platform for many users in this era. Including listening to music as well, the Fungjai application is another option for users who love and like to listen to Thai music. Choose another app to listen to your daily music. But there are still a number of users who want it. In order to bring music that they like to listen to and communicate with friends or acquaintances to listen to, the idea was that Match-Making allows users to listen to music in Fungjai's app to participate. and various activities It's easier to find and exchange ideas in this app. Whether it's finding the right match by sliding the screen in the app. Chat, talk in the chat room. Or even visit each other's stories in the app to make their profiles more interesting.

What does the new feature do?

Adding a self-editing profile in Make the app more interesting by setting Bio to let your partner know how attractive you are.

Choose up to 4 out of 8 music genres. With this new feature, matches will automatically recruit matches that select the number of genres that match you.

Find your favorite match by sliding the screen left and right and double tapping the screen to hit each other's hearts, but to match, the opposite side has to press hearts towards you as well. You can also see what music your opponent is listening to from the artist icon and the currently playing song.

When both sides press the heart and the match is successful. You will be able to chat with each other. Or even go into the story and give your partner heart and he can watch and do too.

Add an uploaded still image or movie. Create stories and attach your favorite songs to it. To make your story interesting for your partner, Matching can see your lifestyle.
